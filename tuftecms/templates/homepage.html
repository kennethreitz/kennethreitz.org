Kenneth Reitz

Creator of Requests, Certifi, Pipenv, Records, Maya & more.

I made Requests, the Python HTTP library downloaded 30 million times a day. I built kjvstudy.org, a free digital study Bible, with AI in two months. I write about what happens when technology meets consciousness, and what we owe each other when we build things people think through.

I live openly with schizoaffective disorder. I'm a husband, a father, and a person of faith. Tech used to be my identity. Now it's craft, not lifestyle.
