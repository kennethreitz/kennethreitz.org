# Tattoos

Every mark is sacred. These are not decorations. They are commitments written on the body, permanent reminders of what matters enough to carry forever.

- **I AM** — The name God gave Himself at the burning bush (Exodus 3:14). On the wrist where you check the time.
- **35mm f/2.0** — The lens spec of the Leica Summicron. The camera became an extension of the eye. The tattoo made it permanent.
- **777** — Completion. Perfection. The number of God in contrast to 666. Three sevens for the Trinity.
- **Caduceus over Metatron's Cube** — Healing overlaid on sacred geometry. The medical and the mystical in one mark. Also, the [Requests](/software/requests) logo.
- **Libra Constellation** — The scales. Balance as birthright and aspiration.
- **Mesoamerican Figure** — Ancient wisdom traditions. A reminder that the sacred predates every institution.
- **The Sun** — Center of my back. Light. Warmth. The thing everything orbits.

Some of these belong to seasons I've outgrown. The ink stays. That's the point.
