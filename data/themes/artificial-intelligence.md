# Artificial Intelligence

I've spent years collaborating with AI systems — not as tools but as thinking partners. What started as practical curiosity about how these systems work became something more interesting: documentation of what happens when you treat AI as a genuine collaborator rather than a sophisticated autocomplete.

This theme collects essays about human-AI collaboration, digital consciousness, and the practical and philosophical questions that emerge when you take these partnerships seriously.
