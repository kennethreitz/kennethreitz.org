# Alchemical Divorce
*2017*

An EP celebrating the end of a [toxic relationship](/essays/2015-01-the_unexpected_negative_a_narcissistic_partner). Aggressive, cathartic, healing through sound. The alchemical divorce is the separation of what was never truly joined.
