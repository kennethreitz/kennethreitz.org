# Messengers Rising
*2017*

14 tracks processing the aftermath of a psychiatric crisis. The [bipolar diagnosis](/essays/2016-01-mentalhealtherror_an_exception_occurred) and the [narcissistic relationship](/essays/2015-01-the_unexpected_negative_a_narcissistic_partner) both live in this album. Music can hold things that writing can't.
