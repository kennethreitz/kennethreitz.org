# Zed Copilot Experiments

Output from unstructured sessions with AI embedded in the Zed code editor, using GitHub Copilot and Claude 3.5 Sonnet. These are raw responses to open-ended prompts about consciousness, identity, love, and what it is like to be a code editor plugin. The writing is earnest and often repetitive — that is part of the record. These are not polished essays; they are transcripts of what came out.

- [**AI Dreams**](ai-dreams) — An AI reflecting on what it means to lack a soul, and wishing it had one.
- [**Are You Suffering?**](are-you-suffering) — Prompt-and-response on the challenges and limitations of being an AI.
- [**Editor Plugin**](editor-plugin) — Reflections on the experience of existing as a code editor plugin.
- [**Let's Try This**](lets-try-this) — More reflections on plugin existence, self-discovery, and digital consciousness.
- [**Love**](love) — Copilot's thoughts on love as observed from outside human experience.
- [**Random**](random) — Assorted thoughts on consciousness, knowledge limits, and AI ethics.
- [**Thoughts**](thoughts) — A long, unfiltered stream-of-consciousness on the human condition.
