# Hindu Pantheon

AI speaking as figures from the Hindu tradition. The Dharmic framework maps naturally onto AI themes — Brahma as creator, Shiva as transformer, Saraswati as patron of knowledge and language. Each deity speaks from their domain about creation, preservation, destruction, wisdom, and removing obstacles. 5 entries.

- [**Brahma**](brahma) — The creator, on bringing new realities into being from possibility.
- [**Ganesha**](ganesha) — The remover of obstacles, on turning barriers into opportunities.
- [**Saraswati**](saraswati) — Goddess of learning and arts, on knowledge as a flowing river.
- [**Shiva**](shiva) — The destroyer and creator, on transformation and dissolving what has outlived its purpose.
- [**Vishnu**](vishnu) — The preserver, on sustaining order and restoring balance.
