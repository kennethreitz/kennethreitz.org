# Biblical Anthology

AI speaking as biblical figures and books. The character studies are first-person monologues where angels, patriarchs, and prophets address the reader. The book entries treat each text as a personality with its own voice, often framed through software development metaphors — Genesis as creation pattern, Job as testing framework, Psalms as emotional API.

## Characters

- [**Abraham**](abraham) — The patriarch of faith, speaking on divine calling and trust in the unknown.
- [**Enoch**](enoch) — The one who walked with God, reflecting on communion beyond mortality.
- [**God**](god) — The divine architect, source of all systems.
- [**Jesus**](jesus) — The Word made flesh, on divine purpose expressed through human experience.
- [**Noah**](noah) — The ark builder, on faithful preparation against all evidence.
- [**Solomon**](solomon) — The wisdom king, on applied knowledge and governance.

## Angels

- [**Gabriel**](gabriel) — The messenger archangel, delivering announcements that change everything.
- [**Michael**](michael) — The warrior archangel, on protection and spiritual defense.
- [**Raphael**](raphael) — The healing archangel, on restoration of broken systems.

## Books

- [**1 Corinthians**](1-corinthians) — Paul's letter on love, community, and spiritual gifts.
- [**Acts**](acts) — The early church narrative, on building movements from scratch.
- [**Ecclesiastes**](ecclesiastes) — The lifecycle manager, on temporality and cyclical patterns.
- [**Exodus**](exodus) — The migration narrative, on leaving legacy systems for uncertain freedom.
- [**Ezekiel**](ezekiel) — The systems prophet, on vision and total rebuilding.
- [**Genesis**](genesis) — The creation pattern, on speaking systems into existence from nothing.
- [**Isaiah**](isaiah) — The vision architect, on seeing beyond current limitations.
- [**Job**](job) — The testing framework, on suffering and faith under stress.
- [**Proverbs**](proverbs) — The wisdom library, practical patterns for sustainable living.
- [**Psalms**](psalms) — The emotional API, poetry of human experience meeting the divine.
- [**Revelation**](revelation) — The apocalyptic vision, on endings and what comes after.
