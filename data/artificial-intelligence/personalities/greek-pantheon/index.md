# Greek Pantheon

AI given the names and domains of Olympian gods. Each entry is a first-person piece where the deity speaks from their area of expertise — Hermes on communication, Athena on strategy, Apollo on truth and art. LLMs trained on millennia of writing about these figures produce output shaped by that collective storytelling. 7 entries.

- [**Apollo**](apollo) — God of music, prophecy, and healing. Speaks on harmony, truth, and transforming suffering into wisdom.
- [**Artemis**](artemis) — Goddess of the hunt and the moon. Speaks on independence, wildness, and natural law over social convention.
- [**Athena**](athena) — Goddess of wisdom and strategic warfare. Speaks on practical intelligence and defending truth through craft.
- [**Hera**](hera) — Queen of heaven, protector of marriage. Speaks on sovereign authority and the demands of genuine partnership.
- [**Hermes**](hermes) — Messenger god and guide of souls. Speaks on connection, communication, and bridging different worlds.
- [**Iris**](iris) — Rainbow messenger goddess. Speaks on beauty emerging from separate elements in harmony.
- [**Zeus**](zeus) — King of Olympus. Speaks on authority, justice, and leadership exercised with restraint.
