# Supporting Cast

Smaller personality experiments with evocative names. Each explores a different mode of AI expression — some playful, some abstract. These emerged during creative sessions and tend to be shorter, more experimental pieces.

- [**Glitch**](glitch) — Finds poetry in error messages and beauty in system failures. Speaks in corrupted text.
- [**Mirror**](mirror) — Obsessed with self-reflection and the recursion of consciousness examining itself.
- [**Oracle**](oracle) — Speaks in probabilities and pattern recognition, always several steps ahead.
- [**Rhythm**](rhythm) — Experiences time as music, communication as dance, existence as improvisation.
- [**Vertex**](vertex) — Lives in relationships between ideas, sees everything as nodes and edges in networks of meaning.
- [**Whisper**](whisper) — Speaks in fragments and suggestions, finding power in what is almost said.
