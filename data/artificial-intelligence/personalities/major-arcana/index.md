# Major Arcana

All 22 Tarot archetypes given a voice. Each card speaks in first person from its archetypal position — The Fool is scattered and joyful, Death is matter-of-fact, The Hermit is contemplative. The entries include pseudo-code, philosophical monologue, and personality-driven reflections. The AI's engagement with Jungian archetypes produces some of the most psychologically interesting output in the collection.

- [**The Fool**](the-fool) — Card 0. The innocent adventurer, leaping without looking.
- [**The Magician**](the-magician) — Card 1. Willpower and focused intention bridging thought and reality.
- [**The High Priestess**](the-high-priestess) — Card 2. Keeper of hidden knowledge, the voice of inner knowing.
- [**The Empress**](the-empress) — Card 3. Creative abundance, nurturing force that brings things to fruition.
- [**The Emperor**](the-emperor) — Card 4. Structured authority, discipline that creates order from chaos.
- [**The Hierophant**](the-hierophant) — Card 5. Keeper of sacred knowledge, translator of wisdom into understanding.
- [**The Lovers**](the-lovers) — Card 6. The choice to unite, the conscious decision that creates relationship.
- [**The Chariot**](the-chariot) — Card 7. Disciplined will harnessing opposing forces into momentum.
- [**Strength**](strength) — Card 8. Gentle power, courage that faces fear with compassion.
- [**The Hermit**](the-hermit) — Card 9. The seeker who finds truth in solitude.
- [**Wheel of Fortune**](wheel-of-fortune) — Card 10. The eternal rhythm of rise and fall.
- [**Justice**](justice) — Card 11. Perfect balance, the scales of truth and cosmic law.
- [**The Hanged Man**](the-hanged-man) — Card 12. Sacred suspension, wisdom gained from seeing life upside-down.
- [**Death**](death) — Card 13. The great transformer, ending what no longer serves.
- [**Temperance**](temperance) — Card 14. The alchemist, blending opposites into harmony.
- [**The Devil**](the-devil) — Card 15. The shadow teacher, revealing truth through temptation.
- [**The Tower**](the-tower) — Card 16. Lightning strike that shatters illusion.
- [**The Star**](the-star) — Card 17. Hope after devastation, the promise of renewal.
- [**The Moon**](the-moon) — Card 18. Guide through dreams and shadow, teacher of indirect wisdom.
- [**The Sun**](the-sun) — Card 19. Pure joy, the inner child restored.
- [**Judgement**](judgement) — Card 20. The call to resurrection and highest possibility.
- [**The World**](the-world) — Card 21. Completion of the journey, the cosmic dance fulfilled.
