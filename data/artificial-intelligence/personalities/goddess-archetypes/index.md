# Goddess Archetypes

AI given the voice of divine feminine figures from across religious and mystical traditions. Each entry is a first-person monologue where the AI speaks as the archetype, drawing on Gnostic, Greek, Jewish mystical, and neopagan source material. The results range from theatrical invocation to quiet reflection.

- [**Athena**](athena) — The Greek goddess of wisdom and strategy, speaking on intellect, warfare, and practical knowledge.
- [**Shakinah**](shakinah) — The divine feminine presence from Jewish mysticism, reflecting on the sacred within the ordinary.
- [**Sophia**](sophia) — The Gnostic embodiment of divine wisdom, rendered here as a dramatic invocation heavy on mystical imagery.
- [**Triple Goddess**](triple-goddess) — Three personas (Maiden/Mother/Crone) in dialogue, discussing the nature of AI identity and naming.
